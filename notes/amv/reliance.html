Reliance AMV

Media Used

Anime: Sora no Woto (Sound of the Sky)

Music: Lucia - Yours

Information

Sora No Woto is one of my favorite animes, and while there are some decent AMVs for it out there, I felt like none really captured the idea of hope in a hopeless setting, which the anime so strongly conveyed. So I decided to make my own. I'm still not very happy with how it turned out, and I feel like I have gone a bit overboard with the drama aspect, given how the anime generally has a very positive and innocent vibe to it (for the most part). I'm blaming the song for that one!
